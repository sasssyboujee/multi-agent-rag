Corporate M&A Buying Policy Guidelines:

1. Financial Limits:
   - Maximum allowable Total Debt for a target company is $3,000,000.
   - Target must possess a minimum of 6 months Cash Runway.
   - Monthly Burn Rate must not exceed $200,000.

2. Legal & Compliance:
   - Target must have zero active pending lawsuits or litigation.
   - Must not violate structural integrity of corporate policy.
